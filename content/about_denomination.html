Community of Christ was born in New York in the 19th century, has been headquartered in Missouri since the 20th century, and now in the 21st century has 250,000 members in over 60 nations. We cherish our history and traditions, but we are also listening for God's spirit to lead us in new directions in an increasingly international and interconnected world.

You can learn more about Community of Christ at cofchrist.org. You may be particularly interested in this page, which outlines enduring principles that describe our identity today (and throughout our history) and mission initiatives that describe what we are trying to do in the world. These—and other helpful information, like the basic beliefs of our denomination—can also be found in this free ebook.
